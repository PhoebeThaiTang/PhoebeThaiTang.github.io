Coroporate Memphis:

Memeification


Considering the amount of hate Corporate Memphis has been generating in recent years, it should come as no surprise that this art style serves as an endless source of memes.

The first Corporate Memphis meme ever, titled “BigTech Corporate Artstyle “StarterPack,” was posted on May 27th, 2020 to Reddit by username ramzes8811. It received 44,500 upvotes in six months.


Then a few months later on November 17th, 2020, this image titled “Share if you fucking hate this art style” using Corporate Memphis appeared:


It immediately went viral and was reposted across social media. According to Know your meme, “Share if you fucking hate this art style” gained over 16,500 retweets and 99,100 likes on Twitter in just a couple of days. On November 18th, an unknown Redditor posted the image in /r/196 subreddit, where it received over 19,400 upvotes in four months.

On November 20th, Twitter user @clayohr posted a Saturn Devouring His Son redraw using Corporate Memphis as the art style.


On the same day, another Twitter user @johnnny2x4 posted a Wojak comic meme that received over 11,500 retweets and 136,300 likes (shown below, right).


Other memes can be found widely across the World Wide Web.
